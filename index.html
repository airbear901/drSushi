Hi! Welcome to the website for Dr. Sushi!

We are a catering business and events company specializing in Japanese cuisine and sustainable sushi bars for private events.

House Calls

Looking for a sushi station at your upcoming event? Look no further!

All we need is room to work! We’ll have fine sushi platters ready for your guests as they arrive and live sushi chefs on site making custom rolls to order.

On-site sushi bar

We’ll come to your house, office, cave, boat, etc. and prepare fresh sushi rolls for you and your guests. We'll have sushi ready when guests arrive and continue to roll during the party. People can come up to us and request special rolls, or choose a variety from the prepared platters at our station. We’ll work with you on an ideal menu or we’ll bring the chef's choice menu (usually california rolls, tuna, salmon, mixed/seasonal veggies, and whatever's fresh at the market). $10 per person for up to two hours. Each additional hour costs $50.

Izakaya night

Izakaya is a Japanese bar that serves delicious small plates of food to accompany your drinks. It’s the perfect place to unwind with company after a long day or to catch up with old friends. If you’ve got a table, why not have us turn your home into an Izakaya for the night? Dr. Sushi and the crew will set up shop, mix up some drinks, and churn out delicious small plates all night while you relax with friends and family.

Washoku - multi-course dinners

Japanese food isn’t always sushi, ramen, and tempura; the majority of Japanese cuisine (Washoku) is based on seasonality, using what’s in season and abundant. Fish served grilled or raw, vegetables pickled or simmered in complex broths, fresh noodles and steamed rice that round out meals - when you hear someone talking about Japanese cooking being so healthy, this is the type of food they’re talking about. Home-style dishes that are consumed on a daily basis. Our midwestern take on Japanese gourmet and comfort foods are what we pride ourselves on most. Washoku dinners start at $10 per course.

Classes

Have you ever wanted to learn how to make sushi?How about in the comfort of your own home? The Doctor offers up his expertise in a hands-on sushi-rolling workshop, where students learn how to roll maki, futomaki, and temaki style sushi. At the end of the lesson, the students become fine diners and enjoy the fruit of their labors; that is to say, they will have their own handmade sushi for dinner! All supplies and ingredients, suitable for omnivores, vegans, and the gluten-free will be provided by Dr. Sushi. You’ll even take home your very own sushi rolling mat after the class! For a list of upcoming sushi classes, check out our eventbrite page. Private classes start at $40 per person.

Sustainability

We are Michigan's first and only sustainable sushi operation. We carefully select all of our fish through communication with our distributors and informed selections based on the Monterey Bay Aquarium Seafood Watch. Our commitment to sustainability will remain with us forever.
